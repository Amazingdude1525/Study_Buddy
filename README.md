## 🌌 The Vision

AI Vityarthi is not just another static task manager. It is a living, adaptive study ecosystem built with a cutting-edge glassmorphic UI. By fusing emotional intelligence algorithms with hardcore productivity tools, this environment reacts to your state of mind to keep you in the flow state. 

*Note: This platform is a continuous work in progress. It will be actively updated with new features, optimizations, and AI models over time.*

> **System Reality Check:** Running real-time sentiment analysis and local AI models concurrently can be resource-intensive. The smoothness of the face-scanning features will depend heavily on your local hardware capabilities, ambient lighting, and webcam quality.

## ✨ Core Architecture & Features

Forget static timers. Here is what powers your new study sessions:

* 🧠 **AI Study Advisor:** Neural study pattern analysis that provides personalized guidance tailored to your actual workflow.
* 🎭 **Real-Time Mood Detection:** Live sentiment analysis via webcam that adapts your study environment's ambient elements based on your emotional state (focus, fatigue, frustration).
* 🎵 **Smart Lofi Streaming:** Mood-synced background frequencies designed to keep you locked in.
* 💻 **Multi-Language Sandbox:** Compile Python, C, and C++ directly within a unified, built-in terminal. Zero context-switching.
* 📊 **Contribution Heatmap:** Visual, GitHub-style tracking of your daily study progress to build unbreakable streaks.
* 🎯 **Dynamic Goal Management:** Track, manage, and systematically conquer your academic milestones.
